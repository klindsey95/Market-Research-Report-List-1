<p><strong>The Composite Fiber Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Parallel Composite Fibres</li><li>Cortical Composite Fibres</li><li>Island Composite Fibres</li><li>Dispersed Primary Fibres</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The composite fiber market is categorized into four main types: </p><p>1. **Parallel Composite Fibers** feature aligned strands for enhanced strength. </p><p>2. **Cortical Composite Fibers** mimic the structure of bone, providing excellent rigidity. </p><p>3. **Island Composite Fibers** have a core-sheath configuration, optimizing flexibility and durability. </p><p>4. **Dispersed Primary Fibers** exhibit a uniform distribution of fibers, enhancing performance in various applications. Each type serves specific needs in industries like automotive, aerospace, and construction, focusing on strength, weight, and application versatility.</p></p>
