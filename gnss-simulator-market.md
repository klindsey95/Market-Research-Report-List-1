<p><p>The GNSS simulator market is categorized into single-channel and multi-channel simulators. Single-channel simulators focus on producing signals for one satellite, allowing for straightforward testing of GNSS receivers under controlled conditions. In contrast, multi-channel simulators generate signals from multiple satellites simultaneously, enabling more complex scenarios and realistic environments for testing. Multi-channel simulators are essential for evaluating receiver performance in various conditions, including urban environments and dynamic scenarios, making them more versatile for advanced applications.</p></p>
