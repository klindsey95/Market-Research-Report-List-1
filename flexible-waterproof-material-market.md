<p><p>The flexible waterproof material market is primarily divided into two types: waterproof sheet material and waterproof coatings. Waterproof sheet materials, such as membranes, films, and fabrics, provide a physical barrier against water infiltration, commonly used in construction and outdoor applications. Waterproof coatings, on the other hand, are liquid products that create a protective layer when applied to surfaces, enhancing their resistance to water damage. Both types play crucial roles in various industries, ensuring durability and protection from moisture-related issues.</p></p>
