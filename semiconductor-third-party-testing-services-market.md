<p><p>The semiconductor third-party testing services market encompasses three key types: laboratory testing, wafer testing, and final testing. Laboratory testing involves assessing materials and components to ensure compliance with industry standards. Wafer testing focuses on evaluating the performance and integrity of semiconductor wafers during production, identifying defects early. Final testing occurs post-manufacturing, verifying that the finished semiconductor devices meet specifications and functional requirements before they are shipped to customers. Together, these services enhance product reliability and performance in the semiconductor industry.</p></p>
