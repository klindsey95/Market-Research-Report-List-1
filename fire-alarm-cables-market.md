<p><p>The fire alarm cables market comprises three main types based on installation requirements. FPL (Fire Power Limited) cables are suitable for general use in fire alarm systems, providing reliable performance. FPLP (Fire Power Limited Plenum) cables are designed for plenum spaces, featuring low smoke and flame characteristics to enhance safety in air circulation areas. FPLR (Fire Power Limited Riser) cables are engineered for vertical spaces, ensuring that they maintain performance and minimize fire spread during emergencies. Each type addresses specific safety and regulatory needs.</p></p>
