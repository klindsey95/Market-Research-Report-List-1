<p><p>Desiccants and adsorbents are materials used to control moisture and adsorb gases or vapors in various applications. Desiccants are hygroscopic substances that absorb moisture from the air, preventing damage in products such as pharmaceuticals, food, and electronics. Common desiccants include silica gel, activated alumina, and molecular sieves. Adsorbents, on the other hand, attract and hold molecules of gases and liquids on their surfaces, playing a crucial role in air and water purification, as well as in gas separation processes.
