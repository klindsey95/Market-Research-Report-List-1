<p><p>Grounding Busbar Trunking Systems are essential for electrical distribution, categorized into segregated and non-segregated bus ducts. Segregated bus ducts feature separate compartments for phases, enhancing safety and minimizing electromagnetic interference. This design allows for easier maintenance and reduced fault risks. Conversely, non-segregated bus ducts utilize a single compartment for all phases, offering a compact solution and lower costs. While non-segregated options are simpler and space-efficient, segregated systems are preferred in high-power applications requiring superior reliability and safety.</p></p>
