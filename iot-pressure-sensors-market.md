<p><ul><li>Absolute Pressure Sensor</li><li>Gauge Pressure Sensor</li><li>Vacuum Pressure Sensor</li><li>Differential Pressure Sensor</li><li>Sealed Pressure Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The IoT pressure sensors market encompasses various types, each catering to specific applications. Absolute pressure sensors measure pressure relative to a perfect vacuum, providing accurate readings in scientific environments. Gauge pressure sensors assess pressure relative to atmospheric pressure, commonly used in tire monitoring. Vacuum pressure sensors measure pressures below atmospheric levels, ideal for HVAC systems. Differential pressure sensors detect pressure differences between two points, essential for flow measurement. Sealed pressure sensors offer a balance, maintaining a reference point for accurate readings in various industries.</p></p>
