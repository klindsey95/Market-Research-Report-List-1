<p><p>A diaphragm valve is a type of valve that utilizes a flexible diaphragm to control fluid flow. This design allows for a tight seal and minimal leakage, making diaphragm valves suitable for a wide range of applications, particularly in industries such as pharmaceuticals, food and beverage, and water treatment. The ability to handle corrosive fluids and offer isolation between the actuator and the flow medium contributes to their popularity.</p><p>The Diaphragm Valve Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 5.80% during the forecast period.
