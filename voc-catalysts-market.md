<p><p>The VOC (volatile organic compounds) catalysts market is segmented into three main types: pellet, honeycomb, and columnar. Pellet catalysts are small, densely packed spheres that offer a high surface area for reactions, making them efficient for various applications. Honeycomb catalysts, characterized by their cellular structure, provide a large surface for catalytic reactions while minimizing resistance to gas flow. Columnar catalysts, typically arranged in vertical columns, facilitate systematic gas flow and enhance reaction efficiency in industrial processes. Each type caters to specific needs and operational conditions.</p></p>
