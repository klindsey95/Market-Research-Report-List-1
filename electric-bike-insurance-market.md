<p><ul><li>Property insurance</li><li>Accident insurance</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electric bike insurance market encompasses two primary types: property insurance and accident insurance. Property insurance covers damages or theft of the e-bike itself, ensuring financial protection for the owner against potential loss. In contrast, accident insurance provides coverage for liabilities arising from accidents involving the e-bike, such as medical expenses for injuries sustained by riders or third parties. Together, these insurance types safeguard both the vehicle and the rider, promoting responsible ownership and usage of electric bikes.</p></p>
