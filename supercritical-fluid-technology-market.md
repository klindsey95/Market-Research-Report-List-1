<p><p>Supercritical Fluid Technology encompasses various market types, each utilizing supercritical fluids for distinct processes. Supercritical Fluid Extraction (SCFE) is used for extracting valuable compounds from raw materials. Supercritical Fluid Reaction (SCFR) facilitates chemical reactions under supercritical conditions. Supercritical Fluid Spray Drying (SFD) efficiently creates powders from liquid solutions. Supercritical Fluid Evolution (SCE) focuses on gas generation from supercritical fluids. Supercritical Fluid Chromatography (SFC) provides advanced separation techniques, while Supercritical Fluid Precipitation (SCP) enables particle size reduction and purification of substances.</p></p>
