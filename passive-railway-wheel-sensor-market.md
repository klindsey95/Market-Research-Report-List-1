<p><p>The passive railway wheel sensor market is categorized into two main types: single wheel sensors and double wheel sensors. Single wheel sensors are designed to monitor individual wheels, providing localized data on wheel performance and condition. In contrast, double wheel sensors are capable of monitoring pairs of wheels, allowing for a more comprehensive assessment of wheel dynamics and alignment. Both types are essential for enhancing rail safety, reliability, and maintenance efficiency by detecting irregularities and wear in real-time.</p></p>
