<p><p>The diamond blades and bits market is categorized into three main types: Surface Set, Electro-plated, and Impregnated. Surface Set diamond tools have diamonds arranged on the surface, ideal for smooth finishes on hard materials. Electro-plated blades feature diamonds bonded to a metal base, offering precision for intricate cuts. Impregnated diamond tools have diamonds embedded throughout the matrix, providing durability and long life for heavy-duty applications. Each type is tailored for specific cutting or grinding needs, catering to various industries.</p></p>
