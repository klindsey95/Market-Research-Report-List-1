<p><ul><li>Socket Weld Connections</li><li>Butt Weld Connections</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pressure seal gate valves market can be classified into two primary types based on connection methods: socket weld connections and butt weld connections. Socket weld connections involve inserting a pipe into a valve socket and welding from the outside, providing a compact design suitable for high-pressure applications. In contrast, butt weld connections require the ends of the pipe to be aligned and welded, ensuring a robust and leak-proof system ideal for larger piping systems. Both types are crucial for ensuring reliability in diverse industrial settings.</p></p>
