<p><p>In the paint booth air filter market, two primary types are prominent: coarse filters and HEPA/ULPA filters. Coarse filters capture larger particles, such as dust and debris, providing initial filtration to protect more sensitive systems. HEPA (High-Efficiency Particulate Air) and ULPA (Ultra-Low Penetration Air) filters, on the other hand, are designed for high-efficiency filtration, removing up to 99.97% of airborne particles, including fine paint overspray and hazardous contaminants, ensuring a cleaner environment for both operators and end products.</p></p>
