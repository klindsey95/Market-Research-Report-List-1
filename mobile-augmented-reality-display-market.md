<p><p>The mobile augmented reality display market encompasses several types, including augmented reality head-mounted displays (HMDs), virtual retinal displays (VRDs), and augmented reality eyewear. HMDs are immersive devices worn on the head, providing users with a fully integrated AR experience. VRDs project images directly onto the retina, offering high-resolution visuals without traditional displays. Augmented reality eyewear resembles conventional glasses but features digital overlays, enhancing the user's view of the real world. Each type caters to different use cases, from gaming to industrial applications.</p></p>
