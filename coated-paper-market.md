<p><p>The coated paper market is primarily categorized into two types: coated chemical pulp and coated mechanical pulp. Coated chemical pulp is produced using chemical processes that enhance brightness, strength, and smoothness, making it ideal for high-quality printing applications. In contrast, coated mechanical pulp derives from mechanical processes, resulting in a more economical option with varied print quality, suitable for less demanding uses. Both types cater to different segments of the publishing, packaging, and commercial printing industries, influencing market dynamics and preferences.</p></p>
