<p><p>The granulator blade market comprises two main types: flat blades and granulation roller blades. Flat blades are primarily used for cutting materials into uniform particles, offering versatility and precision in various applications. In contrast, granulation roller blades operate in a high shear environment, compressing and cutting materials for efficient granulation. Both types cater to different industrial needs, with flat blades often utilized in the chemical and pharmaceutical sectors, while roller blades are preferred in food processing and plastics industries.</p></p>
