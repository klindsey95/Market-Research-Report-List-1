<p><strong>The Automatic Mechanical Watches Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hand Chain Mechanical Watch</li><li>Automatic Chain - up Hand Mechanical Watch</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automatic mechanical watch market can be divided into two main types: hand-chain mechanical watches and automatic chain-up hand mechanical watches. Hand-chain mechanical watches require manual winding by the user, relying on a winding crown to store energy. In contrast, automatic chain-up hand mechanical watches feature a self-winding mechanism that harnesses the wearer's natural motion, allowing the watch to wind itself while also offering the option for manual winding. Both types appeal to enthusiasts who appreciate traditional craftsmanship and mechanical precision.</p></p>
