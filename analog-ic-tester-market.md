<p><p>The Analog IC Tester market comprises two primary types: Wafer Testers and Packaged Device Testers. Wafer Testers evaluate integrated circuits while still on the silicon wafer, allowing for high-volume production testing and enhanced efficiency in detecting defects. In contrast, Packaged Device Testers assess finished integrated circuits that have been encapsulated, ensuring functionality and performance meet specified standards. Both types are essential for verifying the reliability and quality of analog integrated circuits in various electronic applications.</p></p>
