<p><strong>The Liquid Based Needle Free Injector Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>

<p><p>The Liquid Based Needle Free Injector market comprises several types, each utilizing different mechanisms to deliver medication without traditional needles. Jet injectors use high-pressure jets to propel liquid through the skin. Spring-driven injectors utilize a compressed spring mechanism for rapid drug delivery. Laser injectors employ focused laser energy to create microholes in the skin, facilitating drug absorption. Vibration-based injectors use high-frequency vibrations to enhance skin permeability, improving drug penetration. Together, these technologies offer painless alternatives for medication administration.</p></p>
