<p><ul><li>Capacitive Sensor</li><li>Resistance Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Duct air quality sensors, such as capacitive and resistance sensors, monitor air conditions within HVAC systems. Capacitive sensors measure changes in capacitance caused by variations in air properties like humidity or particulate levels, offering high sensitivity and stability. Resistance sensors detect changes in electrical resistance due to air contaminants or moisture, providing cost-effective and reliable measurements. Both types are integral for maintaining indoor air quality, with capacitive sensors excelling in precision and resistance sensors in affordability and durability.</p></p>
