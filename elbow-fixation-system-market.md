<p><strong>The Elbow Fixation System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>External Fixation</li><li>Internal Fixation</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The elbow fixation system market encompasses two main types: external fixation and internal fixation. External fixation involves devices placed outside the body to stabilize fractured bones through pins and rods, allowing for adjustments and easier management of complex injuries. In contrast, internal fixation utilizes hardware like plates and screws surgically implanted within the body to stabilize the bone internally. Both methods are crucial for effective treatment of elbow injuries, improving recovery times, and enhancing patient outcomes.</p></p>
