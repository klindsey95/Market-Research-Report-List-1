<p><p>Anisotropic Magnetoresistive (AMR) sensors are advanced devices that detect magnetic fields by exploiting the anisotropic nature of magnetoresistance. This phenomenon occurs when the electrical resistance of a material changes in response to the orientation of an applied magnetic field. AMR sensors are widely used in various applications, including automotive, industrial, consumer electronics, and medical devices, due to their high sensitivity, accuracy, and robustness. </p><p>The Anisotropic Magnetoresistive (AMR) Sensors Market is anticipated to grow at a CAGR of 7.3% during the forecast period.
