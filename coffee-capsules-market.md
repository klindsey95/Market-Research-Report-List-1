<p><p>The coffee capsules market can be categorized into three main types: aluminum capsules, plastic capsules, and paper capsules. Aluminum capsules are popular for their excellent barrier properties, preserving freshness and flavor while being recyclable. Plastic capsules are lightweight and often less expensive, offering versatility in design and branding but can have environmental concerns. Paper capsules, an eco-friendly option, are biodegradable and compostable, appealing to sustainability-conscious consumers while providing a unique brewing experience. Each type caters to different consumer preferences and environmental considerations.</p></p>
