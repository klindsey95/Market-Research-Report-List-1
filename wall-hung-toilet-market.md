<p><p>The wall-hung toilet market features two primary types: flush toilets and siphon toilets. Flush toilets utilize gravity to expel waste through a pipe, ensuring effective clearing with minimal water use. They are often user-friendly and easy to maintain. Siphon toilets, on the other hand, rely on a siphoning action that creates a vacuum, enhancing waste removal efficiency. This type often offers a quieter flush and better performance in terms of waste clearance, making them popular in modern restroom designs.</p></p>
