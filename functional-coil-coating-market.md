<p><ul><li>Primer</li><li>Back Paint</li><li>Topcoat</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The functional coil coating market encompasses three main types: primer, back paint, and topcoat. Primer serves as the foundational layer, enhancing adhesion and corrosion resistance. Back paint is applied to the reverse side of metal substrates, protecting against environmental factors and improving aesthetics. Topcoat, the final layer, offers durability, weather resistance, and color consistency, making it crucial for achieving the desired finish. Together, these coatings contribute to the longevity and performance of coated metal products in various applications.</p></p>
