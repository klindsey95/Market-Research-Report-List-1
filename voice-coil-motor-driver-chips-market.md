Closed-loop VCM driver chips incorporate feedback mechanisms for improved accuracy and performance in precision tasks, making them suitable for high-end applications like cameras. Optical anti-shake VCM driver chips are specialized for stabilization in imaging systems, reducing vibrations and enhancing image clarity, particularly in handheld devices.</p></p>
