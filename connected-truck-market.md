<p><p>The Connected Truck market includes Dedicated Short Range Communication (DSRC) and Long Range/Cellular Network solutions. DSRC enables real-time, high-speed communication between vehicles and infrastructure over short distances, ideal for safety and traffic management. Long Range/Cellular Networks use cellular technology (e.g., 4G/5G) for broader connectivity, supporting fleet management, telematics, and remote diagnostics over vast distances. Both types enhance trucking efficiency, safety, and operational insights, catering to different use cases based on range and application requirements.</p></p>
