<p><ul><li>Single-stage Hydraulic Torque Converter</li><li>Multistage Hydraulic Torque Converter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The hydraulic torque converter market includes single-stage and multistage types. Single-stage converters use one set of impeller and turbine components, offering simplicity, compact design, and cost-effectiveness, ideal for basic torque multiplication in vehicles and machinery. Multistage converters incorporate multiple impeller-turbine stages, providing higher torque output, improved efficiency, and better performance in heavy-duty applications like construction equipment and industrial machinery. Both types transfer power through fluid dynamics, but multistage converters are more complex and suited for demanding tasks requiring enhanced torque and control.</p></p>
