<p><p>The Enzyme-linked Immunosorbent Assay (ELISA) analyzers market comprises two main types: Optical Filter ELISA analyzers and Optical Grating ELISA analyzers. Optical Filter analyzers utilize specific filters to detect and measure light absorption at predetermined wavelengths, ensuring precise quantification of analytes. In contrast, Optical Grating analyzers employ diffraction gratings to separate light into its component wavelengths, enhancing sensitivity and resolution. Both types serve crucial roles in diagnostics and research, offering reliability and accuracy in measuring concentrations of various biomolecules.</p></p>
