<p><p>Bone meal for oral restorative applications consists of two primary types: Autogenous Tooth Powder and Xenogeneic Beef Bone Meal. Autogenous Tooth Powder is derived from a patient's own dental tissue, offering a high degree of biocompatibility and reduced rejection risk. In contrast, Xenogeneic Beef Bone Meal is sourced from bovine bones, utilized for its osteoconductive properties to support bone regeneration in dental procedures. Both options aim to enhance healing and integration in various oral restorative treatments.</p></p>
