Float glass, created by floating molten glass on molten tin, offers superior clarity and uniformity, making it ideal for high-quality applications. Rolled glass, processed by rolling heated glass, is often used in the manufacture of items requiring curvature or texture. Each type serves distinct purposes across residential, commercial, and industrial sectors.</p></p>
