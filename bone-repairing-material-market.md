<p><p>The bone repairing material market is divided into two primary types: natural bone repair materials and artificial bone repair materials. Natural bone repair materials, derived from natural sources like bioceramics or allografts, integrate well with surrounding tissue and promote healing. In contrast, artificial bone repair materials, such as synthetic polymers and composites, are engineered to mimic bone properties and provide structural support. Both types are essential in orthopedic surgeries and trauma care, aiding in effective bone regeneration and repair.</p></p>
