<p><strong>The Manual Hardness Testers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Rockwell Hardness Tester</li><li>Brinell Hardness Tester</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The manual hardness testers market is primarily segmented into Rockwell and Brinell hardness testers. Rockwell hardness testers measure material hardness by assessing the depth of penetration under a specific load, offering quick results and versatility for various materials. In contrast, Brinell hardness testers use a hardened steel or carbide ball to create an indentation, measuring the diameter of the impression to determine hardness, making them ideal for materials with coarse or uneven surfaces. Together, they cater to diverse industrial applications.</p></p>
