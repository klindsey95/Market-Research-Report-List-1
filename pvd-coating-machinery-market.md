<p><p>The PVD (Physical Vapor Deposition) Coating Machinery market is segmented into three main types: evaporation equipment, sputtering equipment, and others. Evaporation equipment utilizes thermal energy to vaporize materials, allowing them to coat surfaces with thin films. Sputtering equipment, on the other hand, employs ionized gas to eject material from a target onto the substrate. Other equipment may include advanced technologies like atomic layer deposition and pulsed laser deposition, catering to diverse applications across industries such as electronics, automotive, and optics.</p></p>
