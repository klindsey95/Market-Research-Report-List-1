<p><p>The Flight Propulsion System Market is categorized into Air Breathing Engines and Non-Air Breathing Engines. Air Breathing Engines, such as turbojets, turbofans, and ramjets, intake atmospheric air for combustion, making them ideal for commercial aviation and military applications. Non-Air Breathing Engines, like rocket engines, carry both fuel and oxidizer, allowing them to operate in space or high-altitude environments. Each type is tailored to specific flight requirements, affecting performance, efficiency, and the operational range of aircraft.</p></p>
