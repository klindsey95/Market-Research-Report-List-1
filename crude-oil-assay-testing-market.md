<p><p>Crude oil assay testing encompasses various analytical methods to evaluate the properties of crude oil. Total distillation testing assesses the boiling range and yield of distillates, while freeze point testing determines the lowest temperature for crystallization. Smoke point testing measures the burning characteristics of fuels, indicating combustion quality. Total sulfur testing quantifies sulfur content, which impacts corrosiveness and environmental regulations. Additional methods may include viscosity, density, and chemical composition analyses, providing crucial data for refining and market applications.</p></p>
