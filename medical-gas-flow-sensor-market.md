<p><p>The medical gas flow sensor market includes two primary types: flange gas flow meters and plug-in gas flow meters. Flange gas flow meters are typically integrated into piped systems and provide robust, high-accuracy measurements, making them ideal for stationary applications. In contrast, plug-in gas flow meters are designed for portability and ease of use, allowing healthcare professionals to quickly connect and monitor gas flow in various settings. Both types are essential for ensuring accurate gas delivery in medical environments.</p></p>
