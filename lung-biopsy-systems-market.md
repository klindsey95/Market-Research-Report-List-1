<p><p>The lung biopsy systems market encompasses several techniques for obtaining lung tissue samples. Needle biopsy involves using a thin needle to extract tissue from suspicious areas in the lungs, providing quick results with minimal invasion. Thoracoscopic biopsy, performed using a small camera inserted into the chest, allows for direct visualization and sampling of lung tissues. Transbronchial biopsy, utilizing a bronchoscope, enables access to the lung's airways for sampling, making it suitable for less accessible lesions. These methods cater to varying clinical needs and patient conditions.</p></p>
