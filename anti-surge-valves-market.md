<p><ul><li>Pneumatic Anti-surge Valves</li><li>Hydraulic Anti-surge Valves</li><li>Electric Anti-surge Valves</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The anti-surge valves market is categorized into three main types: pneumatic, hydraulic, and electric anti-surge valves. Pneumatic anti-surge valves utilize compressed air for rapid response and precise control, commonly employed in gas and oil applications. Hydraulic anti-surge valves operate using liquid hydraulics, offering robust performance in high-pressure environments. Electric anti-surge valves are driven by electric actuators, providing flexibility and integration with modern control systems, making them suitable for various industrial applications, including chemical processing and power generation.</p></p>
