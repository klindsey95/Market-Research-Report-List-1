<p><p>The cryosurgery units market comprises various types based on the cryogenic agents used. Liquid nitrogen type units are common due to their low cost and effective cooling capabilities. Carbon dioxide type units utilize solid carbon dioxide for targeted tissue destruction. Argon type units employ argon gas for precise tissue ablation through radiofrequency methods. Dimethyl ether-propane type units combine both compounds for enhanced cryogenic effects, offering versatility in treating different medical conditions. Each type caters to specific applications and patient needs.</p></p>
