<p><p>Educational inverted microscopes are categorized into three main types: monocular, binocular, and trinocular. Monocular microscopes feature a single eyepiece, making them ideal for basic education and individual use. Binocular models provide two eyepieces, enhancing depth perception and comfort for prolonged use, which is beneficial in collaborative learning environments. Trinocular microscopes come with an additional eyepiece for camera attachment, allowing for image documentation and sharing, making them suitable for advanced educational settings and research presentations.</p></p>
