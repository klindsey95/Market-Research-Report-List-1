<p><ul><li>Hydrocolloids</li><li>Fibrin</li><li>Collagen</li><li>Hydrogels</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Wound Care Sealants market includes various types such as hydrocolloids, fibrin, collagen, and hydrogels. Hydrocolloids form a gel-like layer upon contact with wound exudate, promoting moist healing and autolytic debridement. Fibrin sealants mimic the body's natural clotting process, aiding in hemostasis and tissue adhesion. Collagen-based sealants support cell migration and tissue regeneration, ideal for chronic wounds. Hydrogels maintain a moist environment, reduce pain, and facilitate healing in burns and ulcers. Each type caters to specific wound conditions, enhancing recovery and minimizing complications.</p></p>
