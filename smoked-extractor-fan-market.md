<p><p>The smoked extractor fan market comprises various types, primarily categorized into centrifugal and axial fans, along with others. Centrifugal fans utilize a rotating impeller to generate high-pressure air, making them suitable for applications requiring strong airflow and higher resistance. Axial fans, on the other hand, move air parallel to the fan axis and are ideal for low-pressure and high-volume airflow needs. Other types include mixed-flow fans and specialized designs tailored for specific industrial applications, enhancing efficiency in smoke extraction.</p></p>
