<p><strong>The Artificial Disc Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Metal</li><li>Biopolymer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The artificial disc market comprises two main types: metal and biopolymer. Metal discs, often made from titanium or cobalt-chromium alloys, provide robust durability and strength, suitable for long-term spinal stabilization. In contrast, biopolymer discs, typically composed of materials like polyethylene or biodegradable polymers, offer a more natural approach, promoting biocompatibility and potentially reducing rejection rates. Both types cater to distinct patient needs, with metal discs favored for their mechanical properties and biopolymer options for their innovative, adaptive characteristics in spinal surgery.</p></p>
