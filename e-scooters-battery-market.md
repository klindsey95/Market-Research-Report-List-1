<p><p>The E-scooter battery market features three main types: Lead-acid, Li-ion, and NiMH batteries. Lead-acid batteries are cost-effective but heavier and have a shorter lifespan, making them suitable for budget models. Li-ion batteries are lightweight, energy-dense, and have a longer life cycle, preferred for high-performance scooters. NiMH batteries offer a balance between the two, providing decent capacity and safety, but are less common due to higher costs and lower energy density compared to Li-ion alternatives.</p></p>
