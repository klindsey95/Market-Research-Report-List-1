<p><p>The oral contraceptive drugs market comprises three main types: long-acting, short-acting, and emergency contraceptives. Long-acting contraceptives, like hormonal implants, provide extended protection with minimal user intervention. Short-acting contraceptives, such as daily pills, require regular adherence for effectiveness. Emergency contraceptives serve as a post-intercourse option to prevent pregnancy, typically used within a short timeframe. Each type addresses different reproductive health needs and preferences, contributing to a diverse and evolving market landscape focused on women's health.</p></p>
