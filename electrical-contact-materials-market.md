<p><p>The electrical contact materials market is primarily divided into silver-based and copper-based types. Silver-based materials are renowned for their superior conductivity, high resistance to oxidation, and excellent wear properties, making them ideal for high-performance applications such as relays and switches. Conversely, copper-based materials offer a cost-effective solution with good conductivity and thermal efficiency, commonly used in lower-demand applications. Both types have distinct advantages, catering to different industry needs, from heavy-duty electrical systems to everyday electronic devices.</p></p>
