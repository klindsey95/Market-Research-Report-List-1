<p><ul><li>Millimeter Wave Radar Chip</li><li>Ultrasonic Radar Chip</li><li>Lidar Chip</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The radar chip market comprises three primary types: Millimeter Wave Radar Chips, Ultrasonic Radar Chips, and Lidar Chips. Millimeter Wave Radar Chips operate at high frequencies, enabling precise distance measurements and are widely used in automotive applications for collision avoidance. Ultrasonic Radar Chips use sound waves for short-range detection and are often found in parking sensors. Lidar Chips utilize laser technology to create detailed 3D maps of environments, being crucial in autonomous vehicles and advanced robotics. Each type caters to specific sensing needs across industries.</p></p>
