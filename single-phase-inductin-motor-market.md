<p><p>The single-phase induction motor market comprises several types, each designed for specific applications. The capacitor-start induction motor uses a capacitor to improve starting torque, making it suitable for heavy-load applications. The capacitor-run induction motor operates with a capacitor during both starting and running, enhancing efficiency and performance. The resistance split-phase induction motor employs a resistance to create a phase shift for starting, ideal for smaller loads. Other types may include shaded-pole and universal motors, catering to diverse industrial and household uses.</p></p>
