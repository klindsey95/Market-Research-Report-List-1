<p><p>The campervan market comprises two main types: Fixed Roof (FR) campervans and Rising Roof (RR) campervans. FR campervans feature a permanent roof structure, offering durability and streamlined design, ideal for compact travel with basic amenities. In contrast, RR campervans have a roof that can be elevated, enhancing interior space and headroom when stationary. This versatility allows for greater comfort during longer trips. Both types cater to different preferences for space, convenience, and mobility in outdoor adventures.</p></p>
