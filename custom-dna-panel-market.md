<p><strong>The Custom DNA Panel Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Amplicon-based Approach</li><li>Hybridization-based Approach</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Custom DNA Panel market features two primary approaches: amplicon-based and hybridization-based methods. The amplicon-based approach focuses on selectively amplifying specific DNA regions using polymerase chain reaction (PCR), enabling targeted sequencing and efficient detection of genetic variations. In contrast, the hybridization-based approach utilizes complementary DNA probes to capture and enrich target sequences from a sample, facilitating comprehensive analysis of various genomic regions. Both methods cater to personalized medicine and research, offering tailored solutions for genetic diagnostics.</p></p>
