<p><p>The thyroid retractor market includes two main types: hand-held retractors and automatic retractors. Hand-held retractors are manually operated tools utilized by surgeons to maintain visibility and access during thyroid surgeries, allowing for precise manipulation. In contrast, automatic retractors are mechanized devices that offer consistent tension and stability, reducing the need for continuous manual support. Both types are essential for enhancing surgical outcomes, improving visibility, and minimizing patient trauma during thyroid procedures.</p></p>
