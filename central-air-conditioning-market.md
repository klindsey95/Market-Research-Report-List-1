<p><ul><li>Vapor Compression Refrigeration</li><li>Absorption Refrigeration</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The central air-conditioning market primarily consists of two main types: vapor compression refrigeration and absorption refrigeration. </p><p>Vapor compression refrigeration relies on a compressor to circulate refrigerant, absorbing heat from indoor air and releasing it outside, making it efficient and widely used in residential and commercial settings. Conversely, absorption refrigeration uses heat energy, typically from gas or steam, to drive the cooling process, making it suitable for areas with limited electricity access or in industrial applications. Both systems cater to diverse cooling needs.</p></p>
