<p><p>Human Resources Management (HRM) software is primarily categorized into two market types: On-Premise and Cloud-Based. On-Premise HRM software is installed locally on a company's servers, giving organizations complete control over the system, data security, and customization, though it requires significant upfront investment and ongoing maintenance. In contrast, Cloud-Based HRM software is hosted online, offering flexibility, scalability, and ease of access from any device. This model typically follows a subscription pricing structure, reducing initial costs and maintenance efforts.</p></p>
