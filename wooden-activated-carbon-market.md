<p><ul><li>Columnar Wooden Activated Carbon</li><li>Granular Wooden Activated Carbon</li><li>Powdered Wooden Activated Carbon</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Wooden Activated Carbon market includes three main types: Columnar, Granular, and Powdered. Columnar Wooden Activated Carbon is cylindrical, offering high mechanical strength and low pressure drop, ideal for gas purification. Granular Wooden Activated Carbon has irregular shapes, providing a balance of adsorption capacity and flow rate, suitable for water treatment. Powdered Wooden Activated Carbon is fine-grained, offering rapid adsorption and high surface area, commonly used in liquid-phase applications like decolorization and chemical purification. Each type caters to specific industrial needs based on form and functionality.</p></p>
