<p><ul><li>Analog Output Hall Sensor</li><li>Digital Output Hall Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Hall elements are sensors that detect magnetic fields and are categorized into two main market types: analog output Hall sensors and digital output Hall sensors. Analog output Hall sensors provide a continuous voltage proportional to the magnetic field strength, making them suitable for precise measurements and feedback applications. In contrast, digital output Hall sensors deliver binary signals indicating the presence or absence of a magnetic field, ideal for position detection and on/off switching applications. Both types serve diverse industrial and automotive markets.</p></p>
