<p><p>The automotive cooling fan market includes three main types: Radiator Fans, Condenser Fans, and Heat/Ventilation Fans. Radiator Fans cool the engine by dissipating heat from the radiator, ensuring optimal engine temperature. Condenser Fans assist in cooling the air conditioning system by reducing refrigerant temperature in the condenser. Heat/Ventilation Fans regulate cabin temperature by circulating air through the heating and ventilation system. Each fan type plays a critical role in maintaining vehicle performance, efficiency, and passenger comfort.</p></p>
