<p><ul><li>Automatic</li><li>Semi-automatic</li><li>Manual</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The handgun market can be classified into three main types: automatic, semi-automatic, and manual. Automatic handguns fire multiple rounds with a single trigger pull and reload automatically, catering primarily to law enforcement and military applications. Semi-automatic models fire one round per trigger pull but automatically load the next round, appealing to both civilians and many professionals for self-defense and recreational shooting. Manual handguns, including revolvers, require manual cycling to load and fire each round, often favored for their reliability and simplicity.</p></p>
