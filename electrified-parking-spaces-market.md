<p><p>Electrified parking spaces can be categorized into two main market types: Single-system Electrification and Dual-system Electrification. Single-system Electrification features a dedicated charging infrastructure, typically for electric vehicles only, ensuring straightforward integration and management. In contrast, Dual-system Electrification combines charging capabilities with other services, such as battery swapping or energy storage, catering to a broader range of vehicle types and enhancing space utility. This diversity in electrification approaches supports the growing demand for efficient electric mobility solutions.</p></p>
