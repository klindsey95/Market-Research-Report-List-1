<p><p>The CPU water cooling radiator market comprises two main types: built-in and external radiators. This type is popular for its space-saving design and streamlined aesthetics. External radiators, on the other hand, are separate units positioned outside the case, allowing for greater cooling capacity and flexibility in layout. They are often used in high-performance systems, where enhanced thermal management is crucial.</p></p>
