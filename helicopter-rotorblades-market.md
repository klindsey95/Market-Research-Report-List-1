<p><p>The helicopter rotor blades market can be categorized into two main types: main rotors and tail rotors. Main rotors are crucial for lift and primary flight control, directly influencing the helicopter's vertical ascent and descent. In contrast, tail rotors provide stability and counteract the torque produced by the main rotors, helping to maintain directional control. Both types are essential for the safe operation of helicopters, impacting design, performance, and maintenance needs across military, commercial, and private sectors.</p></p>
