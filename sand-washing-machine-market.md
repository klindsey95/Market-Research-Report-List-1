<p><strong>The Sand Washing Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Wheel Sand Washing Machine</li><li>Screw Sand Washing Machine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The sand washing machine market primarily comprises two types: wheel sand washing machines and screw sand washing machines. Wheel sand washing machines utilize a circular structure with a bucket wheel to clean and grade sand, removing impurities while maintaining grain shape. In contrast, screw sand washing machines operate with a spiral mechanism that lifts and separates sand particles, effectively washing and dewatering them. Both types cater to various industries, including construction and mining, enhancing the quality of sand for diverse applications.</p></p>
