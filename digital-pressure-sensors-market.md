<p><p>The digital pressure sensor market comprises three main types: relative digital pressure sensors, which measure pressure in relation to atmospheric pressure; absolute digital pressure sensors, designed to measure pressure against a perfect vacuum, providing highly accurate readings; and differential digital pressure sensors, which gauge the difference between two pressure points. Each type serves distinct applications across industries such as aerospace, automotive, and manufacturing, ensuring precise measurements for various operational needs and enhancing system performance and safety.</p></p>
