<p><p>The cannabis extraction market encompasses various methods used to derive cannabinoids and terpenes. Sub/Supercritical CO2 extraction utilizes pressurized carbon dioxide for high-quality, pure extracts. Ethanol extraction employs food-grade alcohol for efficient, cost-effective processing. Hydrocarbon extraction uses solvents like butane, yielding potent concentrates but requiring careful safety protocols. Solvent-less extraction methods, such as rosin pressing, produce extracts without chemicals, catering to health-conscious consumers. Other methods include mechanical and water-based techniques, expanding options for diverse extraction preferences and applications.</p></p>
