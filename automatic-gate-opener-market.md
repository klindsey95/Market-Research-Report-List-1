<p><ul><li>Mechanical</li><li>Hydraulic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automatic gate opener market can be categorized into mechanical and hydraulic types. Mechanical gate openers utilize gears and pulleys for operation, providing reliable performance suitable for lighter gates. They are often more affordable and easier to install. In contrast, hydraulic gate openers employ pressurized fluid to move the gate, offering exceptional power and durability for heavier gates and demanding applications. This type is ideal for commercial and industrial settings, providing smooth operation and enhanced longevity.</p></p>
