<p><p>The Vacuum Pressure Impregnation (VPI) System is a specialized technology utilized for enhancing the durability and performance of porous materials, particularly in the manufacturing of components like electrical insulators and castings. This process involves evacuating air from the material and then applying pressure to force resin or other sealing compounds into its pores, effectively sealing and protecting against moisture and other environmental factors. VPI is increasingly being adopted across various industries, including aerospace, automotive, and electronics, to improve product reliability and longevity.</p><p>The Vacuum Pressure Impregnation System Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 14.3% during the forecast period.
